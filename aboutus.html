About Us

Barangay Group 1 is a model barangay focused on safety, livelihood, education, and digital public service delivery. We support residents through efficient request processing and responsive local governance.

Vision

A peaceful and progressive barangay where every family has access to inclusive services and opportunities.

Mission

To provide fast, fair, and accessible barangay services while promoting unity, accountability, and active citizen participation.
